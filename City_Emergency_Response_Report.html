Processing

UI Layer

React with Tailwind CSS for adaptive layout.

Geographic & Temporal Findings

  • Industrial neighborhood has the highest incident share at 18.92%.
  • Midtown shows high volume yet maintains good response times.
  • Response time averages approximately 8 minutes across neighborhoods.
  • Storm conditions increase response times by ~23% relative to clear weather.
  • Monthly patterns show seasonality with summer peaks.
